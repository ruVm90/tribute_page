Sandor Clegane

The Dog

Sandor is nicknamed "The Dog" for his loyalty to his masters and for following orders without question.

Sandor Clegane is a fictional character from George R. R. Martin's A Song of Ice and Fire saga. Known for his terrifying appearance, his sullen and gruff attitude and his abilities as a warrior.
In the HBO television adaptation of Game of Thrones, the character of Sandor Clegane is played by the actor Rory McCann.
